Banana

Macronutrients (per 100g)
  • Calories: ~89 kcal
  • Carbohydrates: ~22.8 g
    • Sugars: ~12.2 g
    • Dietary Fiber: ~2.6 g
  • Protein: ~1.1 g
  • Fat: ~0.3 g
  • Water content: ~75%
Key Vitamins
  • Vitamin B6 (Pyridoxine) – Supports brain health & metabolism
  • Vitamin C – Antioxidant, boosts immunity
  • Folate (B9) – Important for red blood cell formation
  • Vitamin A (small amounts) – Eye health
Key Minerals
  • Potassium (~358 mg) – Regulates blood pressure, supports heart function
  • Magnesium – Helps muscle & nerve function
  • Manganese – Bone health & metabolism
  • Iron (small amount) – Red blood cell production
Health Benefits
  • Cardiovascular Health – High potassium helps lower blood pressure and reduce heart disease risk
  • Digestive Health – Resistant starch & fiber aid gut health and prevent constipation
  • Energy Boost – Natural sugars + carbs = quick energy source; great for pre/post workout
  • Mood & Brain Support – B6 and tryptophan (converted to serotonin) may improve mood and reduce depression symptoms
Effects on Body Systems
  • Nervous System – B6 supports neurotransmitter function
  • Muscles – Potassium prevents cramps and aids recovery
  • Immune System – Vitamin C enhances defense
  • Digestive System – Fiber promotes regular bowel movement
Potential Risks
  • High sugar – Overconsumption can raise blood sugar, especially in diabetics
  • Latex allergy – Cross-reactivity in sensitive people
  • Migraines – Tyramine in overripe bananas may trigger migraines
Role in Injury Recovery
  • Potassium & magnesium help muscle recovery
  • Vitamin C supports collagen formation (wound healing)
  • B6 helps reduce inflammation and supports metabolism
  • Energy-dense – Helps maintain strength and appetite during recovery
