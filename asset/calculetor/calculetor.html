What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) estimates body fat based on weight and height.

  • Underweight: Below 18.5
  • Normal weight: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25.0 – 29.9

What is BMR?

BMR is the number of calories your body needs at rest to maintain vital functions.

  • Affected by age, gender, height, and weight
  • Men usually have higher BMR
  • BMR decreases with age
  • Helps plan calorie intake
