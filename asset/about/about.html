Now let's talk abt our body

“Training challenges the body. Nutrition repairs it. Recovery transforms it.”

The human body is a powerful system designed for movement, strength, and adaptation. For athletes, performance depends not only on training but on how well the body is fueled and cared for. Proper nutrition provides the energy to train, the building blocks to repair muscles, and the support needed for recovery and injury prevention. When training, nutrition, and rest work together, the body becomes stronger, more efficient, and capable of reaching its full potential.

Each vitamin has a specific job in the body. Vitamin D helps absorb calcium for strong bones, Vitamin C supports healing and immunity, and Vitamin B12 supports nerve function and energy production. Without enough vitamins, the body cannot perform efficiently.

On the other hand :

Minerals work as building blocks and regulators inside the body. Calcium strengthens bones and teeth, iron helps carry oxygen in the blood for energy and endurance, and magnesium supports muscle movement and relaxation. Potassium and sodium maintain fluid balance and proper nerve signals, especially during exercise.
